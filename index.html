// WHO IS HE

The Artist

Roy Shemesh is an Israeli DJ and producer whose craft spans the full spectrum of electronic music and dance. With an ear trained for rhythm and a technical mastery of the decks, his sets are immersive journeys through sound and movement.

From intimate club nights to grand performance stages, Roy brings an infectious energy to every event. His presence across YouTube, SoundCloud, Mixcloud, Instagram and Facebook reflects a community of dedicated fans who follow his music worldwide.
