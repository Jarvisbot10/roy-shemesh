Who Is Roy Shemesh

Roy Shemesh entered the Israeli club scene in the early 2000s, quickly earning the attention of first-class nightlife producers and promoters who invited him to perform at their most exclusive events across Tel Aviv.

His musical identity sits at the intersection of Progressive House, Psytrance, and Progressive Trance — genres that demand both technical precision and an intuitive feel for the crowd's energy.

Beyond the decks, Roy is a producer with official remixes released on Iboga Trance Records and Mutant Disco Records, cementing his place in the international electronic music community.
